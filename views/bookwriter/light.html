The Long, Long Trail

The long-awaited novel by LaResa Darrington

Kate Sorenson thought a job as head cook at a Boy Scout Camp would be the perfect way to make it through summer after her graduation from BYU.

What she didn't expect was the impact it would have on her relationship with her younger sister and secret admirer whose actions bordered on stalking. Can Kate make it through the summer?
